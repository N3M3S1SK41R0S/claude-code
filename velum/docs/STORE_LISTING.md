### Description longue — EN — 2293/4000

```text
One photo is enough to lift the veil on your collection’s hidden value.

VELUM identifies wine, coins, paintings, stamps and watches from guided photos, free text or CSV/JSON imports. It builds a structured analysis sheet and an honest estimate: a stated range, never false certainty.

FIVE SPECIALISED MODULES
• WINE — label and capsule identification, tasting profile, ageing potential, market context and food pairings.
• COINS — obverse, reverse and edge, numismatic identification and a suggested grade with a professional caveat.
• PAINTINGS — always-qualified attribution, condition, provenance and a recommendation to consult a human expert.
• STAMPS — catalogue references, perforation, gum, centring, faults and rarity.
• WATCHES — dial, caseback, movement and clasp; brand, model, reference, calibre, complications, condition, model history and comparable transactions. Authenticity must still be confirmed by a watchmaker.

AN ESTIMATE THAT STATES ITS RELIABILITY
Each item receives a central value in euros, an 80% confidence interval, a wider 95% interval, a reliability score and the sources actually used. When data is missing, VELUM shows that the estimate is unavailable instead of inventing a price.

A DOCUMENTED COLLECTION
Organise your cellar, coin cabinet, gallery, stamp album and watch box. Track values, purchase prices, locations, notes, provenance and alerts. Export object sheets as PDF and your collection as CSV.

YOUR CELLAR’S SOMMELIER
With Gold and Platine, enter a dish: VELUM recommends only bottles already in your cellar. The “drink now” banner flags wines in their optimal window.

ACCESSIBLE AND TRANSPARENT
Senior mode, larger touch targets, VoiceOver support, French and English. Photos are sent to the AI provider only after explicit consent, which can be declined or withdrawn.

PLANS
• Free: 5 scans per week for each module.
• Premium: unlimited scans, exports and alerts.
• Gold: everything in Premium plus the virtual book and cellar sommelier.
• Platine: everything in Gold plus continuous valuation, insurance/estate reports and the collectors’ community.

VELUM estimates are indicative. They are neither a legal appraisal, authentication nor investment advice.

Privacy policy: https://velum.app/privacy
Terms of use: https://velum.app/terms
```
